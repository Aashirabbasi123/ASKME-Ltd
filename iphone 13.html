iPhone 13

The iPhone 13 is the latest flagship smartphone by Apple. It features a stunning design and comes with a range of exciting features.

  • 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with Ceramic Shield front cover
  • A15 Bionic chip with 6-core CPU and 4-core GPU
  • Dual-camera system with 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras
  • Night mode, Deep Fusion, and Smart HDR 4 for impressive photography
  • 12MP TrueDepth front camera with Night mode and Deep Fusion
  • Face ID for secure authentication and Apple Pay
  • Support for 5G connectivity and Dual SIM (nano-SIM and eSIM)
  • iOS 15 with a variety of new features and improvements
  • Available in multiple storage capacities: 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB
  • Colors: Graphite, Gold, Silver, Sierra Blue
