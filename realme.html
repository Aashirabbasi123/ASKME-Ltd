Realme C35 Information

The Realme C35 is the budget smartphone from Realme, featuring a decent design, battery life, and display. The device is priced at PKR 50,200 in Pakistan, and competes with other budget smartphones in the market. While the phone's large size might not be suitable for everyone, it is fairly lightweight and has a depth of about 8mm. The C35 comes with a Glowing Green color variant with matte-finished rails and features a triple-camera setup, with the primary shooter being 50MP, a 2MP macro lens, and a 2MP B&W sensor. While the photos from the phone are good enough, ample details are only visible in strong lighting. The phone houses the Unisoc T616 octa-core SoC, and while the Realme C35 is not really a powerful phone, the battery life is impressive, with a 5,000mAh cell that can be charged at 18W speeds. The Realme C35 comes with Realme's R edition skin, which is more-or-less similar to the RealmeUI. While the phone has some limitations, it is still a solid choice in the budget segment for those looking for a phone with a good design, decent battery life, and a nice display. Design and Display

The Realme C35 has a sleek and stylish design that is sure to impress. The device features a 6.6-inch IPS LCD display with a resolution of 1080 x 2408 pixels. The screen has a waterdrop notch at the top, which houses the front-facing camera. The display has a pixel density of 401 PPI, which makes it ideal for streaming videos, browsing the web, and playing games.

The device is available in two colors, Glowing Black and Glowing Green.

Camera

The Realme C35 features a triple-camera system that includes a 50MP primary camera, a 2MP macro camera, and a 0.3MP depth sensor. The primary camera has an f/1.8 aperture and is capable of capturing sharp and detailed photos. The macro camera is ideal for close-up shots, while the depth sensor, allows the camera to capture portrait shots with a blurred background. The device also features a 8MP front-facing camera, which is perfect for taking selfies and making video calls.
