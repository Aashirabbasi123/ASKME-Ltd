Design and Display

The Realme Narzo 50i has a sleek and stylish design that is sure to impress. The device features a 6.6-inch IPS LCD display with a resolution of 1080 x 2408 pixels. The screen has a waterdrop notch at the top, which houses the front-facing camera. The display has a pixel density of 401 PPI, which makes it ideal for streaming videos, browsing the web, and playing games.

Camera

The Realme Narzo 50i features a triple-camera system that includes a 50MP primary camera, a 2MP macro camera, and a 0.3MP depth sensor. The primary camera has an f/1.8 aperture and is capable of capturing sharp and detailed photos. The macro camera is ideal for close-up shots, while the depth sensor, allows the camera to capture portrait shots with a blurred background. The device also features a 8MP front-facing camera, which is perfect for taking selfies and making video calls.

Performance

he Realme Narzo 50i is powered by a Unisoc Tiger T616 processor, which is a powerful chipset designed to deliver smooth performance. The device also comes with 4GB of RAM and 64GB of internal storage, which can be expanded up to 256GB using a microSD card. The device runs on the Android 11 operating system, which is the latest version of Google's mobile operating system. The phone's performance is further enhanced by the Realme UI 2.0 user interface, which is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.

Battery Life

The device also supports 18W fast charging, which means that the battery can be charged from 0 to 100% in just a few hours.
